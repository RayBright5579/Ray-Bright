About Us


Ray Bright was founded with a simple mission: to help homeowners and businesses get the most out of their solar energy by providing professional, affordable, and eco-friendly solar panel cleaning services. Over time, dust, dirt, bird droppings, and hard water stains reduce the efficiency of your solar panels. Our expert cleaning ensures they stay in peak condition, maximizing your energy output.

  • Professional Team: Our skilled technicians have years of experience in solar maintenance and cleaning.
  • Affordable & Transparent Pricing: No hidden charges – just straightforward, budget-friendly services.
  • Local & Trusted: Proudly serving Rajkot, Gujarat, and nearby areas with high customer satisfaction.
